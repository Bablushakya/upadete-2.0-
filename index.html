Following the journey through India

7-Day Sourcing Itinerary

Day 02

Discover Old Delhi & Acclimate

A gentle day of rest, exploring historical Old Delhi markets, tasting local spices, and acclimating to the rich local environment.

Day 01

Arrival & Welcome

Airport pickup and transfer to boutique lodging followed by an evening welcome dinner and sourcing orientation.

Day 03

IHGF Delhi Trade Show

Explore the massive IHGF Delhi Fair, one of Asia's largest gift and home decor trade shows, to identify potential manufacturing and artisan partners.

Day 04

IHGF Sourcing Depth

A second dedicated day at the trade show for deep-dive supplier meetings, hands-on product evaluations, and custom sampling arrangements.

Day 05

Jaipur Factory Floor Visits

Travel to Jaipur for direct visits with vetted manufacturers, touring facility floors and meeting development and QA teams.

Day 06

Jaipur Artisan Discovery

Explore Jaipur’s artisan communities, block printing workshops, local fabric craft markets, and historical heritage sites.

Day 07

Wrap-Up & Departure

Final debriefing, pre-shipment logistics planning, export consolidation review, and airport transfers for departure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this trip best suited for? expand_more

It is designed for retailers, product developers, brand founders, wholesale businesses, interior designers, makers, and artisans looking to source directly from India.

Do I need sourcing experience? expand_more

No prior sourcing experience is required. Leaf & Loom provides guided assistance, translation, and consultations to help both beginners and experienced buyers navigate the process.

How many people attend? expand_more

To ensure a personalized and high-quality experience, our sourcing groups are kept small, typically consisting of 8 to 12 participants.

Do I need a visa? expand_more

Yes, an Indian visa is required for travel. Most travelers qualify for an e-Tourist or e-Business visa. We provide guidance and assistance during the pre-trip planning sessions.

What should I pack? expand_more

We recommend comfortable clothing suitable for warm weather and factory visits, along with good walking shoes. A detailed packing list is provided before departure.

Will I have free time? expand_more

Yes, the itinerary is designed to balance business and relaxation. We have dedicated discovery days (like in Old Delhi and Jaipur) where you can explore at your own pace.

Can I bring a spouse or business partner? expand_more

Yes, business partners and spouses are welcome. Each participant must submit an application so we can tailor the travel arrangements accordingly.

What is the physical activity level? expand_more

The trip involves a moderate amount of walking during factory tours, market visits, and trade shows. Comfortable walking shoes are highly recommended.

What happens after I apply? expand_more

Once you submit your application, we will schedule an introductory discovery call to discuss your sourcing goals, walk you through the investment structure, and answer your questions.
