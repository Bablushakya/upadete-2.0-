01 / Introduction

Ready to source from India, but don't know where to start ?

That's exactly why this trip exists.

This is a professionally guided sourcing journey to India — built for business owners, makers, retailers, designers, and product brands who want to source directly but don't know where to start.

Why This Trip?

01 . More Than A Sourcing Trip

02 . Explore India

Where Business Meets Beauty

India offers unparalleled design heritage, artisan craftsmanship, and a richness of culture that will inspire your creative vision. Every day holds new possibilities.

03 . Kim Guides Every Step

Never Navigate Alone

Kim has made this journey half a dozen times. She handles the translator, the factory introductions, the cultural nuances — so you can focus entirely on building your business.

Following the journey through India

7-Day Sourcing Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival & Welcome

Airport pickup and transfer to boutique lodging followed by an evening welcome dinner and sourcing orientation.

Day 02

Discover Old Delhi & Acclimate

A gentle day of rest, exploring historical Old Delhi markets, tasting local spices, and acclimating to the rich local environment.

Day 03

IHGF Delhi Trade Show

Explore the massive IHGF Delhi Fair, one of Asia's largest gift and home decor trade shows, to identify potential manufacturing and artisan partners.

Day 04

IHGF Sourcing Depth

A second dedicated day at the trade show for deep-dive supplier meetings, hands-on product evaluations, and custom sampling arrangements.

Day 05

Jaipur Factory Floor Visits

Travel to Jaipur for direct visits with vetted manufacturers, touring facility floors and meeting development and QA teams.

Day 06

Jaipur Artisan Discovery

Explore Jaipur’s artisan communities, block printing workshops, local fabric craft markets, and historical heritage sites.

Day 07

Wrap-Up & Departure

Final debriefing, pre-shipment logistics planning, export consolidation review, and airport transfers for departure.

Logistics, Inclusions & Pricing

What's Included

  • Airport pickup, transfer & private ground transportation throughout India
  • Premium heritage hotel & boutique lodging accommodations
  • Professionally guided factory floor tours & direct artisan introductions
  • English-Hindi translation assistance and cultural bridge support
  • Pre-trip sourcing consultations, luggage planning, and travel orientations

Investment Details

Consultation-Based Pricing

  • • Discovery Call Pricing: Investment details are shared during your introductory discovery call to fit your business goals.
  • • Qualified Applicants: Receive full trip pricing and payment schedules immediately after application approval.
  • • Deposit Process: A refundable placement deposit is required to secure your slot after acceptance.
  • • Payment Schedule: Installment payment terms are available for early registrants.
  • • Cancellation Policy: Details regarding flexible cancellation and refund terms will be provided in your custom trip contract.
*Flexible business financing options may be discussed during your discovery call.

What's Not Included

  • International airfare to and from New Delhi
  • Compulsory international travel & medical insurance
  • Passport fees & Indian visa application costs
  • Personal shopping budget, custom samples, and individual cargo shipping
  • Most daily lunch & dinner meals outside scheduled group dining events
